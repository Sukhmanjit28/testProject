Project Introduction
1.Post
Users can upload their artworks in the form of videos and photos.

2.Like
Allows users to like the content posted.

3.Purchases
Customers can buy/order handmade products online.

4.Rewards
More the activity on the profile, more the number of free gifts will be rewarded to encourage the artists.

5.Interactions
The website allows artists to collaborate with each other via posts.
